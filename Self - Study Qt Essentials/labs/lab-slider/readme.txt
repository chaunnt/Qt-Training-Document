==========
Slider Lab
==========

Create a slider widget, which contains a QSlider and a QLabel.
Your slider widget shall be source compatible with QSlider.

Source compatibility is not only based on class methods, it includes also Signal and Slots.

The main.cpp code is there to test your slider functionality.

Step 1:
=======

Lookup QSlider documentation and the provided methods and Signals and Slots.

Step 2:
=======

Implement the slider widget. It shall present a QSlider with a QLabel.
The QSlider and QLabel shall show the value set on your slider widget.
When the user slides the QSlider, the right signals shall be emitted from your slider.
